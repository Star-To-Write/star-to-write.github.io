EXECUTIVE TEAM

PRESIDENT

JULIA PHILIP

Instagram: @iilovejuliaa

Julia Philip is the founder and president of Star to Write. When creating this nonprofit, she had one thing in mind: to give people a voice.
She wanted to build a platform where anyone could come and share their writing, publish their work, and express themselves for free. Whether it's poetry, articles, stories, or personal reflections — every voice deserves to be heard.
At Star to Write, your words matter; your story matters. So write your heart out, express yourself freely, and be a star to write.
